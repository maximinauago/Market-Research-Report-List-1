Premixed rush coats serve as a quick, thin-layer application for surface preparation, enhancing bonding with subsequent materials. Premixed plasters provide a ready-to-use solution for finishing walls, ensuring a smooth, even surface with minimal mixing, facilitating both efficiency and quality in construction projects.</p></p>
