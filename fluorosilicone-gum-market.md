<p><p>The fluorosilicone gum market is primarily divided into two types: high temperature vulcanization (HTV) and room temperature vulcanization (RTV). HTV fluorosilicone gums are used for applications requiring resistance to extreme temperatures and harsh chemicals, often found in aerospace and automotive sectors. In contrast, RTV fluorosilicone gums cure at ambient temperatures, making them suitable for easier applications in electrical insulation and sealants. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing performance with processing conditions.</p></p>
