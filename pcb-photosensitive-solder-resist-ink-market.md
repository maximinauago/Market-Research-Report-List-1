<p><ul><li>Liquid Photosensitive</li><li>Uv Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB photosensitive solder resist ink market comprises two main types: liquid photosensitive inks and UV-cured inks. Liquid photosensitive inks are typically applied as a coating that hardens upon exposure to light, creating a durable resist layer during the soldering process. In contrast, UV-cured inks are quickly solidified using ultraviolet light, offering rapid processing times and enhanced durability. Both types are essential for protecting circuit patterns and ensuring reliable electrical performance in printed circuit boards.</p></p>
