<p><ul><li>X-ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The full body scanner market comprises two primary types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners utilize ionizing radiation to produce detailed images of the body, effectively detecting concealed objects but raising concerns about radiation exposure. In contrast, millimeter wave scanners employ non-ionizing radio frequency waves to generate images, providing a safer alternative while effectively identifying potential threats. Both technologies serve as crucial tools in security settings, such as airports and public events, enhancing safety and threat detection.</p></p>
