<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty optical fibers market is primarily categorized into multimode and single-mode fibers. Multimode fibers have a larger core diameter, allowing multiple light paths, making them suitable for short-distance communication and higher data transmission rates in local networks. In contrast, single-mode fibers possess a smaller core that supports a single light path, enabling long-distance transmission with minimal signal attenuation, ideal for telecommunications and high-speed internet. Both types cater to specific applications in industries such as telecommunications, medical, and aerospace.</p></p>
