<p><strong>The Flat Fiber Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat fiber cable market is categorized into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode fiber cables utilize a single light wave for long-distance communication, offering high data rates and reduced signal loss. They are ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, Multi-Mode fiber cables transmit multiple light waves simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances, like within buildings or campuses. Multi-Mode cables are generally more cost-effective but are limited in distance and bandwidth compared to Single-Mode fibers.</p></p>
