TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) utilize crystalline materials that glow when heated, providing a measure of cumulative exposure. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) Dosimeters use light to read stored energy in materials, offering real-time monitoring capabilities. Additionally, other types include film badges and electronic dosimeters, catering to diverse applications, ensuring safety and compliance in radiation management.</p></p>
